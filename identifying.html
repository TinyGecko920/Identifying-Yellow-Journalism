Identify Yellow Journalism

How can you idenitfy yellow journalism and what can you do about it?

What Can Be Done?

There is not much that can actually be done about Yellow Journalism. It won't be going anywhere anytime soon, the media is at a constant war for clicks. The best you can do is look out for it and prevent yourself from being fooled. Most yellow journalism today contains clickbait titles that later reveal those titles to be innacruate. An article may contain a clickbait title like "Giant Asteroid Soon To Destroy Earth" just to reveal it only might hit earth in a couple of lightyears.

Identifying Yellow Journalism?

The ability to identify Yellow Journalism can be a very important skill to learn, so how can you do it? The first step is to stop believing everything you see, you should never assume a headline to be true no matter what source it came from. Take your time to read the full story or article. You should search for the facts, and watch out for bias. This skill will prevent you from being potentially misinformed.
